Federal Website Scanner

The Federal Website Scanner offers on-demand analysis of U.S. government websites. The scanner looks for indicators that websites are following best practices. The scanner is currently scanning {{ num_domains }} federal domains.

What the scanner can do

  • Check for the presence of specific files (code.json, robots.txt, sitemap.xml and more)
  • Report on the use of shared services including the Digital Analytics Program (DAP) and the U.S. Web Design System (USWDS)
  • Provide analysis to inform next steps for developers and website managers

Features

  • Scans are updated daily
  • Scans can cover entire domains or individual websites
  • Data is customizable via API
  • Scans are available from anywhere
  • Scans are open to everyone
  • Results are exportable

Help us build

The Federal Website Scanner is a working prototype. Give it a try and let us know what you think. E-mail site-scanning@gsa.gov or work with us on GitHub.
